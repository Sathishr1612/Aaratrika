Reliable Coal & Mineral Transportation Services

Aaratrika Minerals & Transport Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted minerals trading and transportation company, specializing in coal, iron, and bulk mineral logistics solutions. We support industrial and commercial clients with safe, timely, and cost-effective transportation services across India.

With proven expertise in coal transportation, mineral trading, and bulk logistics management, we ensure seamless movement of materials while maintaining high standards of quality, safety, and compliance.

Our focus on operational efficiency and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a reliable partner for mineral transportation and trading services.

Our Services

Comprehensive Logistics Solutions

We operate not just as transporters, but as logistics partners supporting uninterrupted industrial operations.

Rail Logistics Management

End-to-end rail logistics management for domestic coal-based power plants, including siding coordination, rake planning, loading supervision, and dispatch monitoring.

Coal Transportation by Road

Reliable road transportation of coal from mines, ports, or stockyards to plants and sidings with optimized route planning and vehicle sourcing.

Road-Cum-Rail (RCR) Logistics

Integrated RCR logistics solutions combining road and rail movement for cost efficiency, faster transit, and seamless coordination.

Coal Crushing Services

In-mine coal crushing services to meet size specifications, improve handling efficiency, and ensure compliance with plant requirements.

Coal Rake Loading

Professional rake loading operations including manpower deployment, equipment coordination, safety compliance, and loading optimization.

Customized Transport Solutions

Long-term bulk transportation contracts and short-term peak-load logistics support tailored to your specific requirements.
