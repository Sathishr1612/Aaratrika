ABOUT AARATRIKA

About Aaratrika Minerals & Transport Pvt. Ltd.

Aaratrika Minerals & Transport Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted end-to-end industrial logistics solutions provider, managing seamless movement of coal, iron, and bulk materials from mines and ports to plants and finished goods destinations. We specialize in rail logistics management, bulk road transportation, Road-cum-Rail (RCR) coordination, coal crushing, rake loading, and finished goods distribution, supporting uninterrupted supply chains for power, steel, and core industries across India.

Our Direction

Vision Future Forward

To become a leading coal and mineral transportation company known for reliability, service excellence, and sustainable logistics solutions.

We aim to build a scalable logistics network supporting power, steel, and core industries across India, ensuring efficiency, safety, and long-term operational excellence.
