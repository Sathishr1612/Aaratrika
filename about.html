ABOUT US

About Aaratrika Minerals & Transport Pvt. Ltd.

Aaratrika Minerals & Transport Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted minerals trading and transportation company, specializing in coal, iron, and bulk mineral logistics solutions. We support industrial and commercial clients with safe, timely, and cost-effective transportation services across India. With proven expertise in coal transportation, mineral trading, and bulk logistics management, we ensure seamless movement of materials while maintaining high standards of quality, safety, and compliance.

Our Direction

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading coal and mineral transportation company known for reliability, service excellence, and sustainable logistics solutions.

We envision a future where Aaratrika Minerals & Transport Pvt. Ltd. is the preferred logistics partner for power, steel, and core industries across India, setting benchmarks in operational efficiency and safety standards.

Our Mission

  • Deliver safe and timely coal and mineral transportation services
  • Maintain consistent quality standards across all logistics operations
  • Build long-term partnerships with industrial clients
  • Improve operational efficiency through continuous innovation
